About the Villa

Queen's Cottage is a 4-bedroom, 5-bathroom beachfront villa that sleeps up to 8 guests. We are located in the Silver Sands community, just steps away from the Caribbean Sea. Our staff has 25 years of experience combined in Jamaica's bustling hospitality industry, and we have been operating this vacation home since 2020.

Amenities include:

  • Private Infinity Pool
  • Private Chef + Menu Planning
  • Housekeeping and Butler Services
  • Beach View + Access
  • Jogging Trail
  • Excursion Planning
  • Air Conditioning
  • Soaking Tub
  • Kayaks, Paddleboards, Board Games and a PS4
  • 24-hour security

FAQs

The closest airport to us is Sangster International Airport in Montego Bay. Upon booking, you can arrange for your transportation with our house manager. The car ride is approximately 45 minutes.

Unfortunately we do not accomodate pets at Queen's Cottage. However, if you have a certified service animal that accompanies you, this is of course allowed.

We ask for guests to plan for a 3pm check in and an 11am check out. If additional accomidations are needed please feel free to plan with the house manager.

Before your stay you will be able to plan and approve of a menu for your stay. You will be able to request specific groceries and meals.

Groceries are not included in the booking cost, however the service of our private chef is included!
