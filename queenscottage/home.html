Frequently Asked Questions

The closest airport to us is Sangster International Airport in Montego Bay. We will arrange for your transportation and the car ride is approximately 45 minutes.

No pets are allowed at Silver Sands. If you have a certified service animal that accompanies you, this is of course allowed.

We ask for guests to plan ahead for an 11am check in and a 3pm check out. If additional accomidations are needed please feel free to plan with the house manager.

Before your stay you will be able to plan and approve of a menu for your stay. You will be able to request specific groceries and meals.
